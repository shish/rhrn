What's it all about?

Long story short, I was looking at moving to a new town, and I wanted to know which parts of the town would be nice to live in.

Thinking that maybe other people want to know about nice places too, I made an app to collect micro-reviews; hopefully we can collectively review the whole world :3

 

What's the plan?

The plan is to make a heat map, so the nicest countries glow green and the troubled ones glow red; then you can zoom in and see different glows for different towns; then zoom in further and see eg different restaurants in a high street glowing different colours :3

 

What do I add?

Anyplace and everyplace that makes you happy or sad~

Happy examples

  • A lovely view
  • Friendly neighbours
  • Comfortable seating

Sad examples

  • Street fights
  • Bike theft hotspots
  • Noisy late-night factories

 

What if someone else already reviewed a place?

You add your own comments too; then when the happiest places are searched for, the area will get a higher score :-)
